Schedule & Playoffs

Regular season lasts from week 1 to week 14.

The top 2 teams from each division will advance to the playoffs.

The winner of each conference will compete in the Fantasy Bowl during week 16 of the regular season.

The winner of the fantasy bowl gets his name engraved on the "Mini-Lombardi". This trophy is presented to the winning manager at the next season's draft. It needs to be returned to the commissioner 1 month prior to the next season's draft so the next recipient's name can be engraved.

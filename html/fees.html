Fees

As or right now one of the managers plays NCAA D1 baseball.  This means all form of gambling is verboten, as it may get him kicked out of baseball altogether.  As such, this will be a free league until he graduates or drops out for the MLB draft.

Once this happens the fees will be the following:

  • All managers will pre-pay the entry fee of $20.
  • Funds will be used to cover the costs of food during the draft and engraving of the trophy.
  • Remaining funds will be divided up as prize money among the top 4 managers.  First place receives 60% of prize money, 2nd place receives 25%, 3rd receives 10% and 4th receives 5%.

Current Player Value

Salary Cap: $250. Some team caps will be less due to released contracts.

The current player values can be found here.

A rookies value is determined by the round he is drafted for the nfl draft. If a rookie is drafted in the 1st round his value shall be $20, $18 in the 2nd round, $16 in the 3rd round, $14 in the 4th round, and $12 in the 5th. All other rookies are valued at $10. Note that some rookies have a listed value of $1. This is due to the formula I used to calculate all player values, and since they have no stats their value is $1. If you draft a rookie their salary will be adjusted accordingly.

Trades

All trades must be made through the league commissioner and will be posted to league website.

Trades must be agreed upon by all parties concerned at least 48 hours prior to game time for all players involved.

Trades can involve more then 2 teams.

Trades are allowed up to the date any team is mathematically eliminated from advancing to the playoffs.

When trading players the manager must assume the full weight of the contract/value for the player. If I trade Rich Gannon and his current contract is $21/yr then the manager trading for him must be able to absorb a $21 hit. If no contract is in place then current market value is used.

Off season trades are allowed, but not at the previous year's player values. If the value sheet has not been made available yet the trade will be considered "pending" until all player's values have been determined.

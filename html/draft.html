Draft

Draft will be conducted after the "free agent market".

Draft will be conducted in a snake manner.  This means whoever drafts first during round 1 will draft last during round 2.

Draft order shall be determined by the previous seasons standings, with the last place team picking first and the fantasy bowl winner picking last.

No contracted player may be drafted.

At no point during a draft can your salary total go over the salary cap.  If you draft a player who's current value puts you over the cap you have to immediately drop enough players to get under the cap or change your draft choice.

During an expansion draft (new manager to league) the new manager will be allowed to select any 3 non-contracted players.  Once the manager has selected his 3 players the draft will proceed as normal.

Draft shall be a live draft (no auto draft).  Managers who cannot attend can participate via phone.  Managers who cannot participate via phone can send in their draft order.

The format of the draft will most likely change depending on the number of teams participating.

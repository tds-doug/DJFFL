Rosters

Player positions are QB (quarterback), WR (wide receiver), RB (running back), TE (Tight End), K (kicker), and DEF (defense).

Each manager also drafts an NFL coach. This does not count as one of the 14 players.

Total value of all players cannot be greater than salary cap.

If manager wants to change starters for a week he must contact the commissioner prior to the kickoff of the first game for that week. This means that if he wants to bench Kurt Warner who plays at 1PM PST and start Jeff Garcia who plays at 6PM PST, he must still contact commissioner prior to kickoff of first game (usually at 10AM PST). This also means that during Thanksgiving week calls must be placed before kickoff of Thursday's first game.

If a manager chooses to email in changes the email timestamp must be earlier then kickoff of the first game on the Commissioner's machine. It does not matter what time you sent it, but rather what time I received it.

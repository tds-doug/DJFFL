Adding/Droping Players

All transactions must be made through the league commissioner and will be posted to league website.

All requests to add or drop a player must be recieved by the league commissioner by midnight on Wednesday of that week. Transactions will be processed and posted the next day (Thursday).

All transactions that involve adding or dropping a player will take place "simultaneously" on the day after the deadline. If more then one manager wants the same player priority will be determined via the current waiver priority list (posted on the transactions page).

If any transaction puts you over your salary cap it will be discarded.

All transaction requests by a manager must be prioritized. If you want more then one player, indicate which one you want the most, second most, and so on.

Even though all transactions are "simultaneous", a managers requests will be processed serially. This means the transaction with the highest priority will go first, then the second. If you want to add a player who will put you over the cap and then drop a player who will put you back under, the net result will be that you will only be dropping a player. The first transaction will be discarded because it violates the salary cap rule.

Be mindful that other managers may have a higher waiver priority then you and may pick up a player you want. If this results in your team not having a player to fill a position the league commissioner will attempt to contact you. If you are unavailable then your team will not have a player at that position for the week.
